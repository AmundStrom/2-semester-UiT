You need python and pygame installed to play this game

Player 1 (right)
	- turn left: 	a
	- turn right:	d
	- move forwards: 	w
	- shoot: 		left control

Player 2 (left)
	- turn left: 	left arrow
	- turn right:	right arrow
	- move forwards: 	up arrow
	- shoot: 		right control

Players can crash into obsacles and eachother

Players have fuel that deplete when moving forward, 
when fuel reaches 0 player cant move forwards, but can still turn right and left

To refuel touch the green orbs on the top and bottom of the screen
